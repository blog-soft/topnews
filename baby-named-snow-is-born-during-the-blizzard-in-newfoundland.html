Baby named "Snow" is born during the blizzard in Newfoundland

Little Levi Jacob Snow was born on the night of January 17 in Saint-Jean, Newfoundland, when a record blizzard brought more than 76 cm of snow to the territory.
Kyle Snow was very anxious when his wife, Shaundell Dee, started having contractions early Friday morning. Not only was his son only scheduled for the end of the month, but there was also a blizzard waiting for them.
"It was crazy !," said Mr. Snow, in a telephone interview with Le Journal de Montréal, Monday morning. There was no snow when we left the house at 3:30 in the morning. But in the seven minutes it took to get to the hospital, it was the most total blizzard. "
"Obviously, many comments were made to me about the birth of my son because of our family name and the circumstances that led to his birth," he said.
Note that "Snow" means "snow" in English.
